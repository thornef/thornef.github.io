Course information :

Calculus is a beautiful subject dating back to Newton, Leibnitz, and Gauss. A student who completes Math 141 will master concepts and gain skills related to functions and graphs, limits, derivatives, and integrals, and will also learn a variety of applications. The successful student will also refine his/her abilities in problem solving, abstract reasoning, and mathematical writing. The student will also gain introductory exposure to theorems and proofs and the rigorous side of mathematics.
