• Bob -- Mean 3-torsion in the class group of imaginary quadratic fields.

    Obtains the secondary term for a smoothed version of the 3-torsion count, via equidistribution of Heegner points.

  • Manjul, Arul, Jacob -- On the Davenport-Heilbronn theorems and second order terms

  • Manjul and Ariel Shnidman -- On the number of cubic orders of bounded discriminant having automorphism group $C_3$, and related problems

    Pushes the cubic forms method further to study arithmetic properties of the associated cubic rings.

  • (Recommended by Yongqiang) Hirzebruch surfaces and trigonal curves: Chapter 2 of Chapters on algebraic surfaces.

  • (Recommended by Yongqiang) Beauville, `Complex algebraic surfaces'; also Ch. 5.2 of Hartshorne.

  • (Recommended by Yongqiang) Poonen, Sieve methods for varieties over finite fields and arithmetic schemes. Recommended by Yongqiang as being in the same spirit as his work. He uses and extends the techniques there.

  • Takashi and Frank, Secondary terms in counting functions for cubic fields.

  • Frank, Four perspectives on secondary terms in the Davenport-Heilbronn theorems.
